Deed of simple mortgage

Consult Legal Counsel

Seek legal advice to ensure compliance with local laws.

Identify Parties

Clearly define the borrower (mortgagor) and lender (mortgagee).

Property Description

Describe the property being mortgaged.

Loan Terms

Specify the loan amount, interest rate, repayment terms, and maturity date.

Mortgage Terms

Define the mortgage's duration, conditions, and default penalties.

Execution and Notarization

Sign the deed in the presence of witnesses and a notary, if required.

Filing and Registration

Record the deed with the appropriate government office to protect the lender's interest.

Release of Mortgage

Provide for the release of the mortgage upon full repayment.
