Sale Agreement

Identify Parties

Clearly name and provide contact information for the seller and buyer.

Property Description

Describe the property being sold, including the address and unique identifiers.

Price and Payment Terms

Specify the purchase price, payment terms, and any earnest money.

Contingencies

Include conditions like inspections, financing, and repairs.

Closing Date

Set the closing date when property transfer and payment occur.

Legal Review

Consult legal counsel to ensure compliance with local laws and regulations

Sign and Execute

Both parties sign, date, and possibly notarize the release deed

Delivery and Record Keeping

Determine document delivery and maintenance of records.
