Lockout Agreement

Identify Parties

Clearly identify both parties involved, including contact information.

Property Description

Provide a detailed property description.

Lockout Period

Specify the duration of the lockout period.

Price and Terms

Outline the purchase price, terms, and earnest money.

Due Diligence

Define the due diligence period for inspections and reviews.

Exclusivity

State that the seller won't negotiate with other buyers during the lockout.

Legal Review

Consider legal counsel for compliance.

Signatures and Execution

Both parties sign and date the agreement to make it legally binding.
