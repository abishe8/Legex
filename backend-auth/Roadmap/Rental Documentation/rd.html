Rental Document

Contact Information

Provide contact details for both landlord and tenant.

Property Description

Describe the rental property, including its address and any unique features.

Lease Terms

Specify the lease duration, start and end dates.

Rent and Payment Terms

State the monthly rent amount, due date, and accepted payment methods.

Deposit and Rules

Combine the security deposit amount and key rules such as smoking, pets, and noise policies.

Notice Periods

Clearly detail notice requirements for lease termination, entry, or other actions.

Signatures and Copies

Both parties sign and date the agreement, and each receives a copy.

Legal Review

Consider attorney review for compliance with local laws.
